how to learn the optimization?

Optimization is a craft rather than a science. So,

  • Practice more
    • Do not make practical knowledge too theoretical
  • Look that other people do
    • It helps to find a real usages of different approaches and techniques.
  • Dig into the architecture
    • Hardware evolving very fast so todays devices become obsolete very quick. Comprehensive knowledge helps to see beforehand.
